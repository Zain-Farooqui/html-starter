Training Budget and Policy

Objective: To encourage and support the professional development of employees by providing a defined training budget.

Scope: This policy applies to all full-time employees eligible for professional training programs.

Policy Details:

  • Training Budget Allocation: Each employee is allocated an annual training budget of $2,000 for workshops, seminars, certifications, and other role-relevant training.
  • Approval Process: Employees must submit a training request form, which must be approved by their manager and HR.
  • Eligibility: Employees must have completed six months of service. The training program must align with their role.
  • Reimbursement: Receipts and certificates must be submitted for reimbursement. Non-compliance with schedules may result in retraction.
