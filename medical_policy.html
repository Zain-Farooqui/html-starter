Medical Policy

Objective: To provide employees and their dependents access to comprehensive healthcare coverage.

Scope: This policy covers all employees and their immediate family members.

Policy Details:

  • Coverage: Employees are covered for consultations, hospitalization, and prescribed treatments up to $10,000 annually.
  • Network Hospitals: Access to empaneled hospitals for cashless treatments. Non-network hospital bills can be reimbursed within policy limits.
  • Dependents: Coverage includes the spouse, children, and up to two dependent parents.
  • Claims Process: Bills and documentation must be submitted within 30 days for processing.
