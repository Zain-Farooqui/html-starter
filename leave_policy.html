Leave Policy

Objective: To ensure employees have a clear understanding of their entitlements and the leave application process.

Scope: This policy applies to all employees.

Policy Details:

  • Leave Entitlements: 20 annual leave days, 10 sick leave days, and 7 casual leave days per year.
  • Leave Application Process: Leave requests must be submitted through the leave management system and approved by the reporting manager.
  • Leave Carry Forward: Up to 10 unused annual leave days can be carried forward to the next year.
  • Unpaid Leave: Additional leave beyond entitlement will be unpaid.
